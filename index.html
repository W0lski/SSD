South Sudan Consulate General Dubai, UAE

Safeguarding the interests of South Sudanese citizens travelling to, living or working in the UAE city.

Welcome to Our Office

PASSPORT SERVICES

Apply for a new passport or renew the old one.

Passport requirements:

  • Birth certificate or other citizenship evidence
  • Old passport in case of renewal
  • 2 passport size photos
  • Prepare for interview

NOTARIAL SERVICES

Verify your documents with us

Notarial services available include:

  • Authentications and apostilles
  • Validation of signatures on certain documents
  • Certification of copies of original documents

Please note passport, notarial and other consular services are by appointment only.
Fees apply for these servies. Payment must be in UAE Dirhams, by either bank card or cash.


JURISDICTION

The Consulate General of South Sudan in Dubai promotes South Sudan's trade, commercial and investment interests in the UAE city.

The Consulate-General also serves and protects South Sudanese living in, working in, and travelling to Dubai.


MISSION

The consulate has officers and local staff who do the following:

  1. Safeguard the interests of South Sudanese residents and tourists.
  2. Provide and renew South Sudanese passport.
  3. Issue entry visas to South Sudan.
  4. Certify South Sudanese citizenship and marriage documents.
  5. Observe and analyse business developments with this jurisdiction.
  6. Promote South Sudanese culture by implementing and assisting with various cultural events.
  7. Provide general information on South Sudan current affairs as well as official policy to the media and the public.
